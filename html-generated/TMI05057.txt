<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Oklahoma Day invitation and program, Friday, September 16. Program exercises include Music by First Regiment Band, Welcome Address by Silas A. Holcomb, Governor of Nebraska; Response by C. M. Barnes, Governor of Oklahoma; Address by Gurdon W. Wattles; Address by J. C. Roberts; and Address by Major E. A. Woodson, U.S. Army-Indian Agent for Cheyenne and Arapahoe Indians.</pre>
