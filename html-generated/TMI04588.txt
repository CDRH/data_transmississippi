<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Mounted photograph book containing 12  AAC color photographs by F. A. Rinehart. Photographs include: Grand Court, Looking West; Government Building; Nebraska Building; Horticultural Building; Agricultural Building; Mines Building; Neptune's Fountain; The Midway, The Rialto; The Midway--East Avenue; South Facade, Agriculture Building; East End of Grand Court; and Night Illumination of Grand Court. Printed by the Detroit Photographic Company.</pre>
