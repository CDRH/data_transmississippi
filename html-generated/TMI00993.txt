<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Bemis Brothers Bag Company Building, looking northwest. Signage reads "Bemis, Boston, St. Louis, Minneapolis, New Orleans, West Superior, Indianapolis, San Francisco, Omaha." Patriotic bunting mounted above entrance. Two men and a woman walking. Machinery and Electricity Building visible to the northwest. Corner of Montgomery Ward Building to the north.</pre>
