<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Seating Chart for President's Day Dinner on October 12, 1898, held as part of Peace Jubilee week. 160 attendees listed, including President William McKinley. 1 sheet previously folded in half. Printed on cream paper with black ink.</pre>
