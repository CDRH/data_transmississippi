<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Army and Navy Day Menu, from dinner held October 13, 1898, during Peace Jubilee week. Printed with black ink on white stock, with gold edging. U.S. Flag printed in color at top.  Text reads as follows: "Army and Navy Day. Bouillon En Tasse; Celery; Turban of Salmon, Very; Radishes; Olives; Lamb Chops, Senora; French Peas; Sauterne; Old Glory Punch; Roast Chicken, Au Cresson; Lettuce En Mayonnaise; Neapolitan Ice Cream; Cakes; Fruits; Coffee; Cigars; Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition. Markel Cafe. Thursday October 13th."</pre>
