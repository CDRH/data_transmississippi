<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Peace Jubilee booklet. Bound by satin ribbon within transparent paper cover. Includes programs for Mayors' Day, Monday, Ocotober 10; Governors' Day, Tuesday, October 11; President's Day, Wednesday, October 12; Civil Government Day, Friday, October 14; and Children's Jubilee Day, Saturday October 15. Includes photographs and illustrations.</pre>
