<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Scrapbook of clippings of the Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition. Clippings previously mounted on scrapbook pages. Inclues program pages and news articles from Boston Evening Transcript; World Herald. Subjects include The Fountain of Nautilus, souvenir coins, and comemorative postage stamps.</pre>
