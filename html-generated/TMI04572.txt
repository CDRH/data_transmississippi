<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Letter stating that the Omaha Woman's Club will retain $30 for expenses possibly incident to the returning of the Underwood shell collection on temporary display at the Omaha Public Library. Signed by Harriet C. Towne, Mary I. Creigh, Laura Scott, and Edith Tobitt.  1 sheet previously folded in quarters.  Typed transcription. </pre>
