<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character. Document written by G. C. Porter about the character of Gurdon W. Wattles for Omaha Bee newspaper investigation. Accuses Wattles of an extensive history of corrupt business dealings. Typed on cream colored paper. Last page bottom corners torn.</pre>
