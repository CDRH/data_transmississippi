<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character. Letter to Edward Rosewater from Gurdon W. Wattles. Part of Omaha Bee Newspaper investigation. Wattles requests a copy of paper he read in Rosewater's office so that he can address its many mistatements of facts. 1 sheet previously folded in thirds. Handwritten in pencil on blue lined cream colored paper.</pre>
