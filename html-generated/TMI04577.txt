<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Letter to Lewis S. Reed from F. P. Kirkendall. Asks to have a meeting with the Omaha Public Library Board, along with Edward Rosewater and Gurdon W. Wattles, about how best to preserve souvenirs and pictures from the Trans-Mississippi Exposition. 1 sheet previously folded in twelfths.</pre>
