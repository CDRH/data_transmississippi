<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Letter to John A. Wakefield from J. G. Hiestand acknowledging receipt of payment for mineral collection. Includes list of specimens purchased by the Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition committee from J. G. Hiestand and donated to the City of Omaha to be displayed at the Omaha Public Library. Mineral collection includes Blue Quartz Malachite, Malachite Azurite, Malachite Bisbee, Amazon Stone, Topaz, and Phenacite. Contains 2 sheets. 1 sheet previously folded in twelfths; typed transcription. 1 sheet previously folded in 25ths with notes on reverse; typed transcription.</pre>
