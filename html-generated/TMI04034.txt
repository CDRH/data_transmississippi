<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character. Letter to A. J. Sutton from Powers and Hays Law Office. Agrees to procure attainable evidence regarding misrepresentations of  purchase value of Osmond State Bank stock allegedly made by Gurdon W. Wattles. 2 sheets previously folded in sixths. 1 sheet torn across bottom. Handwritten in black ink on lined Powers & Hays notepaper.</pre>
