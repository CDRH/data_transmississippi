<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character. Letter to Herman Kountze and Members of Special Exposition Committee, from Gurdon W. Wattles. Mentions A. W. Swender, G. C. Porter, Frederick Planner, and James Wattles.  Summary of affidavits. 37 sheets previously folded in quarters and 1 envelope. Blue ink on mimeograph paper.</pre>
