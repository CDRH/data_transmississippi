<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Program from Army and Navy Day, Thursday, October 13, 1898, during Peace Jubilee Week. Listed activities include: Exercises, Addresses, Lunch, Parades, Ascension of War Balloons, Shame Battle, and Fireworks. Portraits of Army and Navy officials along border, with illustrations of ships and soliders with cannons in the field during battle.</pre>
