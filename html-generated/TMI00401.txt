<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Bemis Brothers Bag Company Building, looking southwest. Signage reads "Bemis, Boston, St. Louis, Minneapolis, New Orleans, West Superior, Indianapolis, San Francisco, Omaha." Two men and a woman pose at front entrance. Patriotic bunting mounted above entryway. Building statuary includes cats in bags.</pre>
