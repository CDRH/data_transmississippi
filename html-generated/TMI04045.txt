<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Newspaper article; Aspersions on character; Letters from Carroll Iowa. Describes farewell banquet thrown in honor of Gurdon W. Wattles upon his move from Iowa to Nebraska. Recto and verso of article clippings.</pre>
