<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Commutation ticket. 1 sheet previously folded in half; tickets attached. First page is interior of ticket booklet. Contains photo of man with moustache wearing hat, coat, and bow tie and holding a card printed with the number 3058, and four exposition admission tickets, each printed on blue paper with black text, and numbered sequentially. Second page is exterior of ticket booklet, belonging to 3058 D. Printed on white stock with black text.</pre>
