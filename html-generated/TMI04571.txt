<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Letter to John W. Wakefield from the secretary of the Omaha Public Library Board regarding a resolution of thanks passed and recorded in the minutes of their last meeting for the donation of fine art pictures.  Illustrations include: "Fichermans Departure," by Louis Paul Dessur; "Abandoned on New England Coast," by Charles H. Davis; "Opal," by Robert Reid; "Moonrise and Mist," by S. E. Whitteman; "Girl Herding Cows," by E. I. Conse; and "Moonrise Off Cape Ann," by L. H. Meakin." 1 sheet previously folded in thirds.</pre>
