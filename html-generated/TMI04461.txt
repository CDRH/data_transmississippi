<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Notebook for Trans-Mississippi Library Congress at Omaha, Sept. 29th and 30th, and Oct. 1, 1898.  Includes administrative notes detailing decision to implement a congress of librarians during the Exposition, programs, and news articles from the Omaha Bee and the Omaha World Herald.</pre>
