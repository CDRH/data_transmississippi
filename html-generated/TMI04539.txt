<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Lithograph of Grand Court lagoon, looking east. Balustrated Island in foreground, gondolas in water, and Manufactures, Machinery & Electricity Buildings to the Northeast; Twin Tower Restaurants and grand colonnade to the east; and Mines & Mining and Liberal Arts Buildings to the southeast.  Light crowd surrounding lagoon. The Bee Publishing Co., Omaha, Neb. 1 sheet previously folded in quarters. Poster.</pre>
