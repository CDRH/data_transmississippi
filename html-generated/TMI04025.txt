<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character. Letter to Gurdon W. Wattles from W. A. Wyatt. Agreement to give Wattles a copy of paper concerning his character. Enclosed report references his stated net worth of at least $250,000; his properties, stocks and liabilities; and testiment to his high communitity standing.  1 sheet previously folded in twelfths. Typed in blue ink on The Bradstreet Company stationary with signature in black ink. 1 sheet previously folded in sixths. Typed in blue ink.</pre>
