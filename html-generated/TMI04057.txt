<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Aspersions on character; Affidavits from Carroll Iowa. Collection of documents sent during investigation of Gurdon W. Wattles by W. H. Mohr, referencing court cases involving A. W. Swender, and Leola May English, Leola. 1 sheet and attachment previously folded in quarters.</pre>
