<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Promotional pamphlet for Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition. Publicity book for the Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition. Includes images and descriptions of buildings, descriptions of potential attractions, and information about Omaha, Nebraska, for the purpose of advocating the exposition.</pre>
