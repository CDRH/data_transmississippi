<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Official one-cent postal card featuring the Horticulture Building. View 1 of 10  printed by U.S. Postal Card Co. Engraved and printed by Chicago Colortype Co. Includes image of woman pruning shrubbery, signatures of Furdon W. Wattles and John A. Wakefield, and Exposition seal. Verso and recto. Back of card includes portrait of Thomas Jefferson.</pre>
