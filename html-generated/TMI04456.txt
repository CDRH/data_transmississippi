<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Pamphlet for The Burlington Route Exhibit in the Agriculture Building, made by the Land Department of the Burlington & Missouri River Railroad in Nebraska. Includes a photo and description of the exhibit and a narrative about a family on the plains. 1 sheet previously folded in eighths. </pre>
