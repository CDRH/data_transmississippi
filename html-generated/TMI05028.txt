<p>Description:</p>
<pre>Colorized poster. Omaha World Herald Supplement. Depicts the Grand Court and Lagoon, looking west. Mines and Mining, Liberal, and Fine Arts Buildings to the East; U.S. Government Building and Colonnates to the west;  Agriculture, Manufactures' and Machinery and Electricity Buildings to the north, with a crowd of spectators and gondolas in the lagoon. Framed; previously folded in twelfths.</pre>
