<p>Description:</p>
<pre>List of 14 letters regarding donation of Trans-Mississippi Exposition Materials to the Omaha Public Library. Donors include John A. Wakefield, Lewis S. Reed, Harriet C. Towne, Mary I. Creigh, Laura Scott, Edith Tobitt, F. P. Kirkendall, J. G. Hiestand, and David L. Day.</pre>
