<p>Aspersions on character. Letter from Schmidt and Vollmer, Attorneys at Law, to Gurdon W. Wattles. Describes accusations by A. W. Swender that Wattles misrepresented the value of property. Schmidt and Vollmer attest to the falseness of the allegations. 1 sheet previously folded in sixths. Typed in blue ink on letterhead. Signed in pencil.</p>
