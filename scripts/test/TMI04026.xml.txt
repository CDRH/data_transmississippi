<p>Aspersions on character. Letter to Herman Kountz from Gurdon W. Wattles.  Wattles desires copy of report given to committee about his character, and chance to defend himself. References made to G. C. Porter, A. W. Swender, Frederick Planner, and Sumner Wallace. 14 sheets previously folded in thirds. Handwritten in pencil on Union Bank of Omaha Stationary.</p>
