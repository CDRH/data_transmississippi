<p>Aspersions on character. Letter to Gurdon W. Wattles from John McHugh, cashier of State Bank of O'Neill. Recounts conversations with G. C. Porter of the Omaha Bee, warning Wattles that Edward Rosewater is investigating him. 7 sheets previously folded in thirds. Typed in black ink on State Bank of O'Neill, Nebraska stationary.</p>
